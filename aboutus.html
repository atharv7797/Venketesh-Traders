Our Journey

Four Decades of Industrial Excellence

Venketsh Traders began as a modest hardware shop with a singular focus: providing reliable tools to local tradesmen. Over the years, we have evolved into a comprehensive hub for industrial and domestic supplies, specializing in complex irrigation systems and high-capacity electric motors.

Our growth has been dictated by the needs of our community. When the local agriculture sector faced water scarcity challenges, we expanded our irrigation portfolio. When the construction boom required better plumbing standards, we partnered with the world's leading material manufacturers to bring durability to every site.
