Choosing the Right Motor

A specialized 3-step guide to ensure your equipment lasts for decades.

01. Load Requirements

Determine the total torque needed to start and run your application. Overloading leads to premature failure.

02. Power Supply

Match the motor with your site's voltage. Single-phase is common for homes; 3-phase is standard for factories.
